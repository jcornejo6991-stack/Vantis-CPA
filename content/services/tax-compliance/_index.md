  - title: "Tax Filing Done Cleanly and Completely"
    body: |
      Tax compliance is about getting filings done correctly, on time, and with enough review that issues do not get pushed into the future. We handle the filing process from intake through submission, prepare extensions and quarterly estimates when needed, and keep the work tied back to the books and actual facts. Whether you need ongoing annual filings, amended returns, or help responding to a notice, the goal is the same: clean compliance work and fewer surprises later.
  - title: "Individual Tax Filings"
    body: |
      We prepare individual tax returns for straightforward and complex situations, including W-2 income, 1099-NEC self-employment income, itemized deductions, interest and dividend income, rental property reporting, brokerage activity, retirement distributions, equity compensation, and multi-state filings. That includes the 1099 forms that come from stock, bond, mutual fund, and retirement accounts, along with the schedules needed to report gains, losses, dividends, interest, and rental activity correctly. We also handle common equity compensation reporting issues, including RSUs and nonqualified stock options (NSOs). We review the filing carefully, make sure the reporting is internally consistent, and submit it on time with the supporting details in place.

      Common filings include:

      * Form 1040
      * W-2 wage reporting
      * 1099-NEC nonemployee compensation reporting
      * Equity compensation reporting, including RSUs and NSOs
      * Schedule A for itemized deductions
      * Schedule B for interest and dividend income
      * Schedule C
      * Schedule D for stock, bond, and capital gain transactions
      * Schedule E for rental and passive income
      * Schedule K-1 reporting
      * Multi-state and nonresident returns
      * Form 1040-X amended returns
      * Trust and estate filings on Form 1041
  - title: "Business Tax Filings"
    body: |
      We handle business tax filings for entities that need clean books, accurate reporting, and returns that align with the underlying records. That includes annual business income tax returns, quarterly estimates, extensions, and catch-up work when prior years have been missed or filed incorrectly.

      Common filings include:

      * Form 1120 for C-corporations
      * Form 1120-S for S-corporations
      * Form 1065 for partnerships
      * State business income tax returns
      * Quarterly estimated tax payment support
      * Extension filings
  - title: "Notices, Corrections, and Ongoing Filing Support"
    body: |
      Filing the return is only part of the job. We also handle IRS and state notices, amended returns, prior-year catch-up work, and filing corrections when something needs to be fixed. If a notice arrives, we review it, trace it back to the return or filing history, and respond with the documentation needed to close the issue cleanly. If you are behind, we work through the backlog systematically instead of treating every year like a separate emergency.

      This is filing work with follow-through. We do not disappear after submission.
  - title: "Quarterly Estimates and Extensions"
    body: |
      Many compliance problems start before the return is even filed. Estimated tax payments get missed, extension deadlines are misunderstood, or payment planning is left until the last minute. We help calculate quarterly estimates, prepare extension filings, and coordinate payment timing so you stay current and avoid preventable penalties.
