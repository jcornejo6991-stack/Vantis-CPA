  - title: "The Penalty Exposure"
    body: |
      International tax is where most CPAs stop and refer out. We don't. If you're a U.S. citizen with foreign accounts, a foreign investor entering the U.S., or operating across borders, the complexity multiplies and the stakes get higher. One missed filing can cost tens of thousands in penalties before the IRS even opens a case. Missed FBAR equals $10,000 per account per year (automatic penalty). Form 5471 is $10,000 per form. Form 5472 is $25,000. These apply even if there's no tax due.
  - title: "Our Approach"
    body: |
      We handle the full scope in-house. No referrals. We build a comprehensive filing calendar, track every obligation by jurisdiction, and file everything on time with clean documentation. We handle:

      * FBAR and FATCA reporting
      * Forms 5471, 5472, 8865, 3520, 8621
      * Controlled foreign corporation elections
      * Entity classification and check-the-box strategies
      * Foreign tax credit optimization
      * GILTI and Subpart F analysis
      * Pre-immigration planning

      Everything is documented and defensible.
  - title: "Strategic Planning"
    body: |
      Beyond compliance, we handle the planning side. If you're entering the U.S., we help structure the business for the most favorable tax treatment from the start. If you're a U.S. business with foreign operations, we analyze your structure for GILTI exposure, Subpart F inclusions, and foreign tax credit positioning. Pre-immigration planning ensures you're not walking into the U.S. tax system blind. These are decisions that compound. Getting them right early protects you. Getting them wrong is expensive to unwind.
