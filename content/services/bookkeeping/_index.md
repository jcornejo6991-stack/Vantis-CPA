    body: |
      We handle the complete monthly cycle:

      * Bank and credit card reconciliation
      * Transaction categorization and cleanup
      * Accounts payable and receivable management
      * Monthly financial reporting and statements

If you're on another platform (Xero, Wave, FreshBooks), we migrate you and clean up the data. We also reconcile your books to your tax return so there are no surprises at filing time.

Think of it as a fractional CFO relationship, someone who knows your numbers, watches your financials, and keeps your books in shape year-round without the cost of a full-time hire. You get the financial clarity to actually run your business with confidence.
