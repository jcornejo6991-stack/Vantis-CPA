  - title: "Florida Sales Tax Is More Complex Than It Looks"
    body: |
      Florida has no income tax, but its sales tax rules are detailed and easy to get wrong. If you're selling products, providing certain services, renting commercial or residential property, or operating in e-commerce, you likely have Florida sales tax obligations. Most businesses don't find out they're out of compliance until the Florida Department of Revenue sends a notice. By then you owe back taxes, interest, and penalties. Getting compliant before that happens is always cheaper.
