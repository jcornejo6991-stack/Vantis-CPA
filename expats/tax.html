Common Situations

We've seen this before

These are the scenarios expat clients bring to us most often. If yours sounds familiar, you're in the right place.

Situation 1
American in Germany unsure whether FEIE or Foreign Tax Credit is better
Germany's high income tax rate often makes the Foreign Tax Credit more valuable than the FEIE — but it depends on your income level, housing costs, and how long you plan to stay abroad. We model both and show you the numbers before you choose.
Situation 2
Dual-income couple with one spouse working abroad
Mixed-source households require careful coordination. We handle the MFJ vs. MFS filing comparison, the partial exclusion for the abroad-based spouse, and treaty implications for the US-based income — so neither of you overpays.
Situation 3
First year abroad with partial US and foreign income
The year you move is the most complex. Establishing bona fide residence or physical presence, handling a split-year return correctly, and making the right elections in year one sets the foundation for every year that follows.
