We help foreign businesses and investors enter the U.S. market with confidence. Whether you are looking to acquire U.S. equities, purchase real estate, or establish a U.S. branch or subsidiary, we provide the tax and compliance structure to do it correctly. The U.S. tax system operates very differently from most jurisdictions, and that gap is where costly exposure is created.

Our priority is protecting you from IRS enforcement and eliminating double taxation — whether through tax treaty positions, proper entity structuring, or estate planning. Penalties for missed or incorrect international filings generally range from $10,000 to $25,000 per form, per year. We also work with U.S. single-member LLCs (SMLLCs) owned by foreign persons, which carry specific disclosure and reporting requirements that are frequently overlooked.

From initial structure through ongoing compliance, we offer a full suite of tax, accounting, and advisory services to keep your U.S. operations on solid ground.
