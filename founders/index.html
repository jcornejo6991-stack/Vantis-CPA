Common situations we handle

The issues most CPAs miss — or don't even know to look for.

Compliance
Form 5472 — Foreign-Owned US Entity

If a foreign person owns 25%+ of your US LLC or C-Corp, you must file Form 5472. Penalties start at $25,000 per violation. Most CPAs don't file it — and most clients don't know they need it.

83(b) Election — Startup Equity

You have 30 days from receiving restricted stock to file an 83(b) election. Miss the window and you could owe ordinary income tax on equity that vests years from now. One missed deadline. Significant consequences.
