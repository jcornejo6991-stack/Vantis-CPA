Common Situations

We've seen this before

Scenario 01

Remote founder needing a US address

You formed a Delaware C-Corp but you live abroad. Your registered agent address isn't accepted by banks or the IRS as a business address. We provide a real US address that works everywhere.

Scenario 02

New LLC with no bank account

You've been mixing personal and business expenses for six months because you couldn't get a business bank account open. We introduce you to Mercury or Relay and get you set up within days.

Scenario 03

Startup needing notarized documents for investors

Your investor's counsel needs notarized copies of your formation documents and a board resolution — by Friday. We handle it in person or via remote online notarization.
