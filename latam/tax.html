Common Situations

Families we've helped

Your situation is specific. Here are some of the cross-border scenarios we work through regularly.

🇻🇪

Venezuelan family with US rental real estate

A family based in Venezuela owns a condominium in Florida. Rental income is US-source income requiring a 1040-NR. We handle the return, optimize deductions, and analyze treaty benefits to reduce withholding on gross rents.

🇨🇴

Colombian investor selling US property

A Colombian national sells a US property and the buyer withholds 15% under FIRPTA. We file Form 8288-B to request a reduced withholding certificate and recover excess amounts through the 1040-NR filing.

🇲🇽

Mexican business owner with a US LLC

A Mexico-resident entrepreneur holds a US LLC generating US-source income. We analyze the treaty position, structure the entity correctly, and file the required US return to minimize tax on US-connected income.
