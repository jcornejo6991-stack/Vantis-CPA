Common Situations

Infrastructure problems we solve

The practical side of maintaining a US entity from abroad is often underestimated. Here are the situations we handle regularly.

🌎

Latin American investor needing a US address for banking

A foreign investor's LLC needs a US address for IRS correspondence and bank account opening. We provide a real business address and the supporting documentation the bank requires to open the account.

⚠️

Foreign-owned LLC dissolved for missing an annual report

A Latin American family's Florida LLC was administratively dissolved after missing a $138 annual report. We file the reinstatement, restore good standing, and implement a reminder system so it never happens again.

🏢

Family office with multiple US entities

A family office maintains four US LLCs across two states. We serve as registered agent for all entities, track every annual report deadline, and provide centralized good-standing monitoring across the portfolio.
