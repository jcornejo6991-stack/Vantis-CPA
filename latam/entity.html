What's Included

Deliverables & Services

From formation through annual compliance — the complete entity lifecycle for Latin American investors with US holdings.

🏢

US LLC Formation for Foreign Investors

State filing, registered agent, EIN application, and ownership documentation structured correctly for non-US members from day one.

🏛️

C-Corp Formation for Operating Businesses

Delaware or Florida C-Corp formation for Latin American entrepreneurs building US-market businesses, with proper foreign ownership documentation.

📋

Foreign-Owned Entity Compliance

Annual Form 5472 filing for foreign-owned US disregarded entities, and Form 1120-F for foreign corporations with US income — avoiding the $25,000 penalty.

🪪

ITIN Applications for Non-US Owners

Preparation and submission of Form W-7 for non-resident owners who need an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number for US tax compliance.

📄

Operating Agreement & Ownership Structure Review

Review and drafting of operating agreements to ensure ownership structure, member rights, and tax classification elections are correctly documented.

📬

Registered Agent Service

Florida registered agent for service of process and official state correspondence — required for every active US entity.

📅

Annual Report & Renewal Management

Annual report filing tracking and submission for all active entities, with deadline reminders to prevent administrative dissolution.

Common Situations

Families and investors we've helped

Entity structure questions are rarely simple for foreign investors. Here are situations we navigate regularly.

🇻🇪

Venezuelan family acquiring US real estate in an LLC

A family acquires Florida condominiums through a US LLC for liability protection and privacy. We form the entity, apply for the EIN, and establish the annual compliance calendar — including Form 5472 each year.

🇲🇽

Mexican entrepreneur forming a US C-Corp

A Mexico-based software entrepreneur wants a Delaware C-Corp to contract with US clients. We form the entity, document foreign ownership properly, and advise on the tax implications of US-connected income.

🇨🇴

Colombian investor evaluating entity structure

A Colombian investor who bought US property individually wants to evaluate whether to transfer it to an LLC. We analyze the tax and FIRPTA implications of the transfer before making any structural recommendation.
