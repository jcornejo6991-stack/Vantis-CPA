S-Corp Election Timing

Converting your LLC to S-Corp taxation is one of the most impactful tax moves for profitable small businesses. But there's a deadline — March 15 for most — and missing it costs you another full year. We evaluate whether S-Corp makes sense for your numbers and file it correctly.
